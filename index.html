Aol offers a safe, judgment-free space to address issues like anxiety, depression, trauma, or domestic violence. Our experienced counselors and Independent Domestic Violence Advocates (IDVAs) provide personalized guidance to help you through tough times.

Support for Domestic Violence & Mental Wellness

Struggling with domestic violence or mental health challenges? Aol provides free counseling for individuals aged 14+ and expert support for those facing abusive situations, empowering you to take control of your well-being.

Emergency Support Guidance

While Aol doesn’t offer a 24/7 crisis line, we can direct you to appropriate resources in emergencies. If you’re in immediate danger, please call 999. For non-urgent support, our team is available to assist outside regular hours.
